Transform Learning with a Local Curriculum

The foundation for effective teaching and learning lies in clarifying what students need to learn. In simple terms, curriculum is the plan for what is taught in your schools. It outlines the knowledge, skills, and concepts that students are expected to acquire at each grade level.

Curriculum unifies initiatives across the district. When navigating initiatives that relate to instruction, assessment, grading and reporting, student engagement, professional learning communities, or one of the many others in the educational world, look to your local curriculum for the answers.
